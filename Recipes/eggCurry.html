Egg Curry

A wonderful mixture of grated eggs and sauted vegetables in a gravy form

  1. Pour some oil onto a pan. Keep it for heating and then add a reasonable thick slice of butter into it
  2. Next, add garlic, ginger, and green chillies to the pan and mix until a uniform texture is observed.
  3. Add chopped onions to your mixture and stir well until you observe a whittish-pink color to it
  4. Then blend some tomatoes and add them to the mxiture in the pan
  5. Add your masalas and mix well before adding boiling water to get the gravy like mixture
  6. Lastly, grate boiled eggs onto your mixture and there you go, have fun!
